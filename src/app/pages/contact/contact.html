FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A standard website takes 2–4 weeks; a full mobile app or ERP takes 8–16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines after discovery.

How is pricing structured?

We offer fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and time-and-material engagements for evolving requirements. No hidden fees, ever.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We're a remote-first team and work with clients across the globe. Communication is async-friendly with weekly syncs.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes — we provide maintenance retainers covering bug fixes, updates, performance monitoring, and feature additions after launch.
